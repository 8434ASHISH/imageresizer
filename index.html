About Us

ResizeFast.AI is a free online tool developed with love in India to help users quickly resize their images without any hassle. Our mission is to provide a simple, efficient, and user-friendly solution for image resizing needs. We are a team passionate about technology and aim to enhance your digital experience!

How to Use

  1. Click the "Choose File" button to upload your image.
  2. Select the resize option: "Resize by KB" or "Resize by Pixels."
  3. Enter the desired KB value or pixel dimensions (width x height).
  4. Check "Maintain Aspect Ratio" if needed.
  5. Choose the output format (JPEG, PNG, or WEBP).
  6. Click "ResizeFast" to process, then download the image or convert to PDF.
